  ## What To Bring:
  <ul class="list-disc list-inside text-gray-800 dark:text-gray-200">
    <li> House hold Junk</li>
    <li> Metal</li>
    <li> Electronics</li>
    <li> Microwaves</li>
    <li> Block Styrofoam - <span class="font-bold">no peanuts or film</span></li>
    <li> Clear #1 plastic clam shells</li>
    <li> Clear plastic bag - <span class="font-bold">bread bags, zip locks</span></li>
  </ul>
</div>

<div class="mb-8">
  
  ## What <span class="font-bold">Not</span> To Bring:
  
  <ul class="list-disc list-inside text-gray-800 dark:text-gray-200">
    <li>Painted Items</li>
    <li>Any construction/remodeling waste materials</li>
    <li>Yard Debris, Kitchen waste, dirt or stumps</li>
    <li>Commercial or hazardous waste </li>
    <li>Concrete, constrictors loads, any foam or asbestos</li>
    <li>Batteries</li>
    <li> Demolition debris (Tiles, Plaster, Shingles, Ceiling, Insulation, Electrical wire, Fireproofing) </li>
    <li>Starch or Styrofoam peanuts, PE, PP Foam</li>
    <li>Clothing, Shoes, or Fabrics</li>
  </ul>
